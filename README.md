Sales Executive Hiring Announcement Poster
1. Topic / Problem
. Designing a professional hiring announcement poster for the company
. The poster announces that the company is actively looking for a new Sales Executive
. Created to attract suitable candidates through social media platforms
3. Why This Is Important
. Increases the company's visibility to job seekers actively looking for opportunities
. Makes it easier for the right candidate to discover and apply for the position
. Helps the company reach a wider pool of applicants beyond traditional job boards
. Bridges the gap between the company's needs and the most suitable candidate
4. What I Learned
. Not every design needs to look different or unique each time
. In a professional setting, simplicity and clarity are more effective than overly decorative designs
. Keeping the poster clean, readable, and on-brand is the right approach for corporate recruitment
. Simple design communicates the message more directly to the target audience
5. Tools / Techniques
. Tool used: Canva
. Technique: Basic editing including layout design and visual composition
6. How We Can Use This in Our Company
. Post the hiring poster on Instagram and other social media platforms
. Reach a broad,  audience of potential candidates
. Reinforce the company's professional brand image to anyone who encounters the post
. Reuse this poster format as a template for future hiring announcements
7. References
. All design and layout decisions were made independently based on personal creativity
8. Time Spent
. Total time invested: 2.5 hours
This includes research, designing, and refining the final poster
